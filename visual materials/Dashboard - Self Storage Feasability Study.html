⚠️ Risk Identification & Mitigation

Every private equity venture carries risk. Below are the primary threats identified for a self-storage launch in Central Luzon, alongside our required strategic mitigations prior to capital deployment.

🏒

Real Estate Lease Risk

Because we are leasing the shell building, the landlord may refuse renewal or severely hike rents after the 3-year initial term.

Mitigation: Utilize modular, demountable storage partitions. Negotiate a locked lease with a pre-agreed 2-year extension option.
📢

Market Adoption Risk

Outside Metro Manila, paying for personal storage is a relatively new concept. Customer acquisition costs may be initially high.

Mitigation: Hyper-targeted digital marketing to condo residents in Angeles. Offer "1st Month Free" trials to induce habit formation.
🔒

Operational Liability

Security breaches, pest damage, or climate-related damage to tenant goods could result in severe brand damage and liability.

Mitigation: Require mandatory tenant insurance. Allocate 15% of CapEx to enterprise-grade CCTV, access control, and ventilation.
