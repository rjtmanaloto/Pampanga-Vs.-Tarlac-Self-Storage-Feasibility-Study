<h1>Pampanga Vs. Tarlac: Self Storage Feasibility Study</h1>


<h2>Goal</h2>

As a sales analyst/strategic consultant, analyze the market to open a new Storage Rental business in Region III (Central Luzon).

<h2>Objectives</h2>

1. Compare Pampanga and Tarlac to see which province has more customers and higher profit potential.
2. Create a logo for "Domain Extension" and a picture of what the storage building should look like.
3. Create new visual materials to explain the business idea to investors.

<h2>Insights</h2>

1. Pampanga, specifically Angeles City, is the better choice. It has many people living in apartments who need extra storage space.
2. The business needs 120 small storage units. Renting 48% of the units will cover our costs, while renting 80% will get our money back in 24 months.
3. There are very few storage businesses in Central Luzon, so we can be among the first to offer this service.

<h2>Workflow</h2>

1. Ask ChatGPT to write the perfect prompt for AI market research along with clarifying questions.
2. Answer the clarifying questions.
3. Use the new prompt in Gemini to complete the market research. Make sure to use the "Deep Research" option.
4. Use the research results to create a webpage, an infographic, and an audio summary using Gemini.
5. Use Gamma to create a PowerPoint presentation based on the research.
6. Ask Chatgpt to write the perfect prompt for designing the Domain Extension company logo. 
7. Use Gemini and the ChatGPT prompt to create the logo. 
8. Take a screenshot of a specific location in Angeles City, Pampanga, using Google Maps. 
9. Use Gemini to create a storage building picture based on your Google Maps screenshot.
10. Add the logo and storage building picture to the Gamma PowerPoint presentation.

<h2>Tools Used</h2>

- <b>Chatgpt</b> 
- <b>Gemini</b> 
- <b>Gamma</b> 

<h2>Environments Used</h2>

- <b>Windows 11</b>
